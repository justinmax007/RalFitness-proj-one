Meet Ralston

Certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist (NSCA-CSCS)

Are you new to fitness? Want a personal fitness trainer at your own convenience? Or just want to focus on your healthy living and diet? I’m Ralston D’Souza, your personal health and fitness coach. I’m a certified strength and conditioning specialist (NSCA-CSCS). I’m also an online health and fitness coach and a personal trainer. Right from a young age, I have taken part in and won Physique Athlete Competitions like Jerai Classic 2016 (2nd place), Musclemania 2014 (top 5), BodyPower 2016 (2nd place), BodyPower India 2017 etc. I’m an active YouTuber through my fitness channel where I promote an active and healthy lifestyle through constant guidance on several aspects of health and fitness.
